About Antipodal Points

An antipodal point is the point on the Earth's surface that is diametrically opposite to a given point. It's the place you would reach if you could tunnel straight through the center of the Earth.

Most antipodal points from land end up in the ocean, as land makes up only about 29% of Earth's surface.
